About Us

Nurturing a Greener World, One Plant at a Time.

Our Journey

Our journey started with a small spark of curiosity and a deep love for nature. Growing up surrounded by lush gardens and towering trees, plants have always been a part of our lives. However, it wasn’t until we moved into our first tiny apartment that we truly understood the impact that greenery can have on a living space and on our well-being.

In those early days, our living room windowsill became our sanctuary. We began with just a few succulents, but as our collection grew, so did our passion. Every new leaf unfurling felt like a small victory, bringing a sense of tranquility and accomplishment to our busy urban lives.

That’s how Planto was born—with a mission to make plant care easy and enjoyable for everyone, regardless of experience. We began by offering a selection of low-maintenance plants, perfect for any space, and quickly grew into a thriving community of plant lovers.

Today, we’re proud to help people transform their homes with greenery, one plant at a time. Whether you’re just starting out or looking to expand your collection, we’re here to support you on your journey.

Join us on this green journey, and let’s grow something beautiful together.
